Grants

  • 2022 – Present — Situation Aware Perception for Safe Autonomous Robotics Systems (ELLIIT Project C08, Linköping University). PhD researcher.
    Applying uncertainty quantification to computer vision tasks to improve the safety and trustworthiness of autonomous systems, including regression, human pose estimation, and end-to-end autonomous driving.
  • 2026 – 2027 — Risk Assessment in Vision-Language-Action Models for Automated Vehicles (Vinnova FFI, Linköping University). PhD researcher.
    Developing risk assessment methods for vision-language-action models in automated driving. Funded by Vinnova (Diarienummer: 2026-00800) under the traffic-safe automation programme.
